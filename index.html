League Rules

Rules & Regulations

All players are expected to know and abide by league rules. Refs have final authority on all calls.

Game Format

  • Two 20-minute halves, running clock
  • Clock stops final 2 minutes of 2nd half (within 5 pts)
  • 5-minute overtime (sudden death on tied games)
  • Each team gets 2 timeouts per half
  • 10 personal fouls allowed before player disqualification
  • Game ball provided — Wilson Evolution

Eligibility

  • Players must be 35 years or older
  • Valid government ID required to verify age
  • Signed liability waiver required before first game
  • Players may only roster on one team per season
  • Minimum 5 players required to start a game
  • Rosters lock after Week 2

Uniforms & Equipment

  • Team jerseys required — no duplicate numbers
  • No jewelry that could be hazardous to other players
  • Basketball shoes required — no casual sneakers
  • Knee braces with exposed metal must be covered
  • Headbands, compression sleeves permitted

Conduct & Sportsmanship

  • Zero tolerance for fighting — immediate season suspension
  • Technical fouls: 1 = warning, 2 = ejection
  • Ejected players must leave the gym immediately
  • Verbal abuse of referees results in immediate ejection
  • League commissioner decisions are final
  • All disputes submitted in writing within 24 hours

Standings & Playoffs

  • Win = 2 pts, Loss = 1 pt, Forfeit = 0 pts
  • Tiebreakers: head-to-head, point differential, points scored
  • Top 4 teams advance to playoffs
  • #1 and #2 seeds receive Play-In byes
  • Playoff seeding finalizes after Week 7

Forfeits & Scheduling

  • Teams must notify by 5 PM day-of to avoid forfeit
  • Forfeit = 0 pts, opponent receives 2 pts
  • Two forfeits in a season may result in disqualification
  • No reschedules after Week 4
  • Makeup games at commissioner's discretion only
